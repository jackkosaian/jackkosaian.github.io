I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am fortunate to work with Rashmi Vinayak as part of the Parallel Data Lab.
My research interests fall broadly within computer systems and networks. My current focus is on resource-efficient reliability in the context of systems used for machine learning inference.

I was previously an undergraduate at the University of Michigan, where I worked with Mosharaf Chowdhury.
