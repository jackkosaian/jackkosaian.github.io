I am a third year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University where I am fortunate to work with Rashmi Vinayak as part of the Parallel Data Lab.
My research interests fall broadly within computer systems and networks.

Previously, I was an undergraduate at the University of Michigan where I worked with Mosharaf Chowdhury on various systems-y projects.
