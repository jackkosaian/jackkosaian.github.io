I began working at NVIDIA on CUTLASS in October of 2022.

I completed a Ph.D. in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was fortunate to work with Rashmi Vinayak as part of the Parallel Data Lab.
My thesis research investigated the interplay between computer systems, machine learning, and coding-theoretic tools.

Prior to graduate school, I was an undergraduate at the University of Michigan, where I worked with Mosharaf Chowdhury.
